Name: Goatman

Description: The Goatman is described as a seven-foot-tall hybrid creature, part man and part goat. Often credited with the deaths of dogs in Prince George's County, Maryland, area.

Location: The Goatman has been spotted in Maryland, Pennsylvania, and Texas.

Danger Level:

10/10. The Goatman is very strong and fast. If you see it, just run and hide.
