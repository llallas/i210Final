Name: Fresno Nightcrawler

Description: The Fresno Nightcrawlers appear to be relatively short creatures (approx. 1.5 meters) with most of their height being made up of their legs, as they possess an extremely small upper body. It is hard to find details in the upper body of the cryptid due to the poor quality of the footage. It is an extremely thin, white humanoid with no arms.

Location: The Fresno Nightcrawlers were originally sighted in Fresno, California, but they have also been seen in Yosemite National Park. Some think these are aliens, so they can be everywhere on Earth.
