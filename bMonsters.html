Name: Black Annis

Description: The Black Annis is a witch who is said to haunt the countryside of Leicestershire, England. Locals know her as "Black Annis' Bower." She was said to be very tall, vicious, and to have blue skin, glowing eyes, long, sharp teeth, and claw-like fingers, which she used to tear into the small children that she has taken.

Location: The Black Annis is said to be living in a dark cave located in the area of Dane Hills.

Danger Level:

10/10 The creature is very aggressive and is capable of disappearing into the trees.
